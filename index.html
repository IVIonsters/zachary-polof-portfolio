About Me

Hello! My Name is Zachary Polof and I am an entry level full stack developer!

I currently work full time in the automotive industry. I have always had a passion for technology and design from a young age, originally going to school for 3D autocad and design.

Originally developing my programming skills on a self taught path using Zero To Mastery as well as many other online resources. After a few months trying the self taught route I opted to enroll in the University of Central Florida's online bootcamp to gain a more direct experience route in several languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, API's, Jquery, Node, Express, MySQL, React among others!

I'm set to graduate later this year in 2024 and look forward to a bright future in programming. My main interest's lie in full stack development , web design, with a follow up in the future into cloud architecture(hopefully).
